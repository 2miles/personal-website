    About Me

    My name is Miles Whitaker. After recently acquiring a bachelors of science in mathematics from PSU I realized I wanted a career in software development or data science. I felt like computer science in general was the perfect blend of building tangible things while also utilizing the theoretical and analytical abilities I gained from my experience with mathematics. Instead of strictly self-learning, I continued attending PSU in order to get a computer science degree.

    Although I currently have no professional experience in software, through the CS curriculum at PSU I became quite proficient in C++ data structures, OOP, and recursive algorithms. Meanwhile in my free time I learned the basics of Python and some of its libraries by making small projects like games and websites. The websites I built using the Django web framework for the front-end and PostgreSQL for the database.
